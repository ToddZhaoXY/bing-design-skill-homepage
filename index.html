07 — Tips

Getting the most from your skill.

01

Plan Mode First

Use Plan Mode to describe your requirements clearly before execution. This significantly reduces errors and back-and-forth iterations.

02

Work in Steps

Break tasks into stages — get the structure right first, then fill in real data later. Incremental progress beats one-shot perfection.

03

Write a Good CLAUDE.md

When working on a dedicated project, a well-written CLAUDE.md gives the AI persistent context and dramatically improves output consistency.

04

Accept Minor Variance

Small detail differences are normal — not every deviation is a skill bug. Prompts in English tend to yield more predictable results.
